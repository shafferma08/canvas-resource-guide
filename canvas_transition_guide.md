## 2. Secure Your "Just in Case" Files
*While not required for Canvas to run, it is a smart safety measure to keep a personal copy of your original Blackboard files.*

### üìÇ Download via Content Collection
The easiest way to grab *all* your files at once in Blackboard:
1.  In your Blackboard course **Control Panel**, click **Content Collection**.
2.  Click the first ID link (e.g., `s2-454-b07...`).
3.  Scroll to the bottom, near the pagination arrows, and select **Show All**.
4.  Check the box at the very top to select all files.
5.  Click **Download Package**.
*This will save a ZIP file to your computer with every handout, image, and document from your course.*
[[Guide: Download Files]](https://help.blackboard.com/Learn/Instructor/Original/Course_Content/Course_Files/Download_Files)

### üìä Archive Your Gradebook
We recommend downloading a copy of your current Grade Center as an Excel file, just so you have a permanent record.
1.  Go to **Grade Center > Full Grade Center**.
2.  Click the **Work Offline** button (top right corner) and select **Download**.
3.  Click **Submit**, then **Download** again to save the Excel file to your computer.
