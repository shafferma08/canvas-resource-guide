Secure Your "Just in Case" Files

It is a smart safety measure to keep a personal copy of your original Blackboard files.

üìÇ Download via Content Collection
The easiest way to grab your files in Blackboard:
  1. In your Blackboard course Control Panel, click Content Collection.
  2. Click the first ID link (e.g., s2-454-b07...).
  3. Scroll to the bottom, select Show All.
  4. Check the boxes for the specific files you need.
  5. Click Download Package.
This will save a ZIP file to your computer.
üìä Archive Your Gradebook
If you want a copy of your current Grade Center for your permanent records:
  1. Go to Grade Center > Full Grade Center.
  2. Click the Work Offline button (top right corner) and select Download.
  3. Click Submit, then Download again to save the Excel file to your computer.
üìÑ Generate Individual/Class Grade Reports
  1. Access Grade Center: Go to Control Panel > Grade Center > Full Grade Center.
  2. Create Report: Select Reports in the action bar, then click Create Report.
  3. Customize Report: In the Header Information area, select items to include.
  4. Select Users: Choose to include All Users, a specific group, or selected users.
  5. Submit and Print: Click Submit, then select Print to save as a PDF or print a hard copy.
