I logged in, but I don't see my PTC course.
If you are logged in but do not see your Pinellas Technical College course on your dashboard, we may need to merge your accounts.
Please email me immediately so I can fix this for you.
Do I use my high school login?
Yes. Since you are a Dual Enrollment student, you continue to use your standard PCSB credentials (R2.D2), just like you do for your high school classes.
I'm getting a "Not a Tenant" error.
This error usually happens when you use the wrong login link (like the PTC student link). Start over and use the specific link above (ending in /login/saml/211).
The login isn't accepting my email.
Make sure you are using your @pcsb.org email address.
Do not use your personal email (Gmail, iCloud, etc.) or a @myptc.edu email.
I forgot my password.
Since this uses your district account, you will need to follow standard PCSB password reset procedures or contact your school's Tech Support for assistance.

If you have any issues that these steps don't resolve, please email me.
