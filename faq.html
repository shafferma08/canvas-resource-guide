My student says they can't log in. What should I check?

This is usually due to one of two reasons:

  1. Wrong URL: Ensure they are using the specific PTC student link: https://pcsb.instructure.com/login/saml/213. The standard "pcsb.org" link often fails for PTC students.
  2. Wrong Credentials: Students must sign in using their full @myptc.edu email address, not a personal email or just their username.
I am both an instructor and a student (e.g., Taking professional development). Which login do I use?
Use the login link that matches your primary role for the accessing session. Generally, staff credentials (pcsb.org) work on the staff URL (.../211), even for PD courses.

üì¶ Content Migration & Setup

I imported my Blackboard course, but it looks messy. Do I have to fix everything now?
No. Prioritize functionality over aesthetics for Feb 16. As long as students can click on Modules and find their assignments/files for this week, you are good. You can clean up the formatting later.
Where are my "Content Folders" from Blackboard?
Canvas does not use nested folders like Blackboard. Instead, it uses **Modules**.

Think of a Module as a "Container" or "Folder" that holds pages, files, and assignments. Using Modules is the #1 way to organize your Canvas course effectively.

Why do my Announcements show as "Unidentified User"?
Announcements imported from Blackboard often lose their "author" metadata. It is recommended to delete old announcements and post fresh ones in Canvas so students know they are current and active.

I see a "Grade Sync" button. Should I click it?
No. Grade sync to Focus is not available for PTC. Please ignore any buttons or settings related to syncing grades.
Action Required: Continue to enter final grades manually in Focus as you always have.
How do I back up my course files & grades?
Crucial Rule: Never treat an LMS (Blackboard or Canvas) as your only storage.
1. Download all important Files from Blackboard content folders.
2. Go to the Blackboard Grade Center and choose "Work Offline > Download" to save a permanent Excel copy of your students' grades.
Once Blackboard access ends, this data will be inaccessible.
Classic Quizzes vs. New Quizzes: Which should I use?
When you create a quiz, Canvas may ask you to choose.
  • Classic Quizzes: Familiar, robust, works best if you just imported from BB. Recommended for now if you need speed.
  • New Quizzes: More modern features but a slightly different interface.

If your imported quizzes are behaving strangely, try creating a comprehensive "Test" assignment manually for temporary use.

I can't find "Student View".
In Canvas, the "Student View" button is located in the top-right corner of the Home page (look for the eyeglass icon).
Tip: You must "Leave Student View" (bottom right) to return to editing.
